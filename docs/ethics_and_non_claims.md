\# Ethics and Non-Claims



This repository is a research prototype for analysis and reporting.



\## Non-claims

\- Not a battery management system (BMS)

\- Not validated for safety-critical use

\- No guarantee of preventing failures or hazards

\- Outputs are informational and require expert review



\## Intended use

\- Research, benchmarking, exploratory analysis, reproducible experiments
